About Collages

Collages are curated collections of music on RED and OPS. When you convert a collage, red-plex creates a matching collection in your Plex library with albums found in your music library.

  • Convert: Create a new Plex collection from a collage ID
  • Update: Refresh existing collections with new albums
  • Fetch Modes: Choose how albums are matched (by filename or metadata)
